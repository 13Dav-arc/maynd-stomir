Maynd Stomir Agency Terms

Pricing & Service Terms

Complete transparency on our dispatch fees, quotes, and payment policy.

1. Standard Call-Out & Diagnostic Fee

Every requested dispatch incurs a mandatory baseline fee of QAR 150. This non-refundable fee guarantees partner dispatch, covers operational travel expenses, and includes up to 60 minutes of on-site inspection or basic labor.

Mandatory Baseline

2. Transparent On-Site Quotes

If repairs require specialized hardware parts or extended labor hours, our technician will assess the scope and submit an official quote directly to Maynd Stomir operations. You will receive an official digital invoice prior to any work execution.

3. Material Sourcing Fee

When technicians are required to leave the site to acquire specific replacement parts from suppliers, a flat QAR 50 Material Sourcing Fee is applied to cover logistics, fuel, and handling time.

100% Cashless Escrow Security

To protect both client quality and platform guarantees, technicians are strictly prohibited from collecting cash or negotiating prices on-site. All transactions are settled digitally via Maynd Stomir secure payment links.
