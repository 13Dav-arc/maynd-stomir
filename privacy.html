Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register as a freelance technician on Maynd Stomir, we collect personal identity credentials strictly necessary to complete our mandatory manual review verification process[cite: 3]. This includes:

  • Full Name, Email Address, and Mobile Phone Number[cite: 3].
  • Qatar ID (QID) numbers and physical document scans[cite: 3].
  • Trade skill certifications and professional licenses (e.g., Kahramaa Card Photos)[cite: 3].

2. Document Security & Storage

All sensitive identity documents (QID cards and Kahramaa licenses) are securely streamed directly into isolated, encrypted root cloud storage buckets. These documents are never exposed publicly and are strictly used by our administrative team during the onboarding vetting pipeline.

3. Data Usage Rules

Your uploaded identity verification records are used solely to confirm your operational credentials inside Qatar. Approved technicians are securely integrated into our proximity-based GPS matching network to receive client service requests.
